📘 Mathematics

📐 Geometry

Geometry studies shapes, angles, lines and figures used in mathematics and design. It uses tools like protractor and scale. Angles are one of the basic chapters in geometry.

📊 Algebra

Algebra uses symbols and equations to solve mathematical problems and unknown values.

📏 Trigonometry

Trigonometry studies triangles using sine, cosine and tangent functions.

⚛️ Physics

🌎 Gravity

Gravity is the force that attracts objects towards Earth and keeps planets in orbit. The theory of gravity was first introduced by Sir Isaac Newton.

⚡ Electricity

Electricity is the flow of electric charge used to power devices and machines. Electricity has 2 charges negative and positive.

🔊 Sound

Sound is a form of energy produced by vibrations travelling through air.

🔬 Chemistry

🧪 Periodic Table

The periodic table arranges chemical elements based on atomic number and properties. For Example, Sodium-Na Lithium-Li Calcium-Ca Copper-Cu Silver-Ag Gold-Au Hydrogen-[H]

⚗️ Acids Bases and Salts

Acids are sour, bases are bitter and salts are formed through chemical reactions. When acids and bases are mixed they form salts. This process is called neutralization.

🌍 Geography

🏔️ Mountains

Mountains are large elevated landforms formed through tectonic plate movements. Mount Everest is the highest mountain in the world. Forests which are found in mountains are called Montane Forest.

🌊 Oceans

Oceans cover most of Earth’s surface and help regulate climate and weather. There are 5 oceans in the world they are Pacific ocean, Atlantic ocean, Indian ocean, Arctic ocean, Southern ocean. Pacific ocean is the largest ocean in the world.

🌧️ Climate

Climate describes long-term weather patterns of a region over many years.

📖 English

✍️ Grammar

Grammar helps form correct sentences and improves communication skills. Topics which are important: Tenses, Nouns, Pronouns, Vowels, Adjectives.

📚 Vocabulary

Vocabulary refers to the collection of words used in a language.

📝 Essay Writing

Essay writing helps students express ideas clearly and creatively.

🚀 Space

🛰️ ISRO

ISRO is India’s space agency known for missions like Chandrayaan and Mangalyaan.

Black holes are regions in space with gravity so strong that even light cannot escape.

The Solar System includes the Sun, planets, moons and other celestial objects.
